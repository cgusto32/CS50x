All About Avocados

Avocados are a nutrient-dense fruit with healthy fats, fiber, and a wide range of vitamins and minerals. Whether you're spreading them on toast, blending them into smoothies, or making guacamole, avocados are a delicious and versatile ingredient!

Fun Facts

  • Avocados are also called "alligator pears" due to their shape and bumpy green skin.
  • They are high in potassium, even more so than bananas!
  • Mexico is the world's leading producer of avocados.
  • Avocados are technically berries with a single seed.

My Favorite Avocado Recipe

I love making a simple avocado toast. Here's how:

  1. Toast a slice of your favorite bread.
  2. Mash half an avocado and spread it on the toast.
  3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and a dash of red chili flakes.
  4. Top with a poached egg for extra protein!
